## Software Engineer (IC2-IC3)

### Structure

**Title:** [Senior/Mid-Level/Staff] Software Engineer
**Level:** IC2/IC3/IC4
**Department:** Engineering
**Location:** Remote/Hybrid/Onsite

**About the Role** (2-3 paragraphs)
- What they'll build
- Impact and scale
- Team structure

**What You'll Do** (4-6 bullets)
- Build features for [specific product area]
- Write clean, maintainable, well-tested code
- Participate in code reviews and technical discussions
- Collaborate with Product and Design on specs
- [IC3+] Mentor junior engineers
- [IC4+] Drive technical decisions across teams

**What We're Looking For**

Required:
- X+ years building production software
- Strong CS fundamentals (data structures, algorithms, systems design)
- Experience with [primary stack: e.g., React, Node.js, PostgreSQL]
- Track record of shipping features users love

Nice to Have:
- Experience with [secondary tech]
- Open source contributions
- Previous startup experience

**Compensation**
- IC2: $120K - $150K
- IC3: $150K - $200K
- IC4: $200K - $280K
- Equity: 0.1% - 0.5%

## Account Executive (Sales)

### Structure

**Title:** [Enterprise/Mid-Market] Account Executive
**Level:** IC2/IC3
**Department:** Sales
**Location:** Remote/Hybrid/Onsite

**About the Role**
- Deal size and sales cycle
- Target market (SMB, mid-market, enterprise)
- Quota expectations

**What You'll Do**
- Own full sales cycle from lead to close
- Hit $[X]M annual quota
- Run discovery, demos, negotiations
- Partner with Sales Engineering and Customer Success
- Build pipeline through outbound prospecting and inbound leads
- Forecast accurately and manage pipeline in Salesforce

**What We're Looking For**

Required:
- X+ years in B2B SaaS sales
- Track record of hitting/exceeding quota
- Experience selling $[deal size] deals
- Consultative selling approach

Nice to Have:
- Industry expertise in [vertical]
- Previous startup experience
- Technical background

**Compensation**
- Base: $100K - $140K (IC2), $140K - $180K (IC3)
- OTE: $200K - $280K (IC2), $280K - $400K (IC3)
- 50/50 base/commission split
- Equity: 0.05% - 0.2%

**Quota:** $1M - $2M ARR (IC2), $2M - $4M ARR (IC3)

## Using These Templates

1. **Choose the right template** for the role
2. **Fill in the brackets** with specifics
3. **Adjust level** (IC2 vs IC3 changes scope and comp)
4. **Customize** based on company stage and needs
5. **Remove what doesn't apply**

These are starting points, not rigid structures. Adapt to your company's voice and needs.
