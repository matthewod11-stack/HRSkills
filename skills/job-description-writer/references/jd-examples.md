## Example 1: Senior Software Engineer (IC3) - Remote

```markdown
# Senior Software Engineer at Acme Analytics

**Location:** Fully Remote (US time zones)
**Level:** IC3 (Senior)
**Department:** Engineering (Platform Team)
**Salary Range:** $150,000 - $200,000
**Equity:** 0.1% - 0.3%

## About the Role

We're looking for a senior engineer to help us build the data analytics platform
used by thousands of companies to make better decisions.

You'll work on our platform team, building the core infrastructure that powers
real-time data processing, visualization, and alerting. You'll own features from
design to deployment, mentor junior engineers, and help shape our technical direction.

This is a high-impact role - the code you write will process millions of events
per day and directly affect how our customers run their businesses.

## What You'll Do

- Build and scale our data ingestion pipeline (handling 10M+ events/day)
- Design and implement new analytics features with Product and Design
- Write clean, well-tested code that your teammates can maintain
- Participate in code reviews and technical planning
- Mentor IC1-IC2 engineers on the team
- Contribute to architecture decisions and technical strategy

## What We're Looking For

### Required
- 5+ years building production web applications
- Strong fundamentals: data structures, algorithms, system design
- Experience with our stack (or similar): Node.js, React, PostgreSQL, Redis
- Track record of shipping features users love

### Nice to Have
- Experience with high-throughput data systems
- Open source contributions
- Previous startup or high-growth environment experience

## Compensation & Benefits

**Base Salary:** $150,000 - $200,000
**Equity:** 0.1% - 0.3% (4-year vest with 1-year cliff)
**Benefits:**
- Health, dental, vision (100% covered for employee)
- 401(k) with 4% match
- Unlimited PTO (team averages 4 weeks/year)
- $2,000/year learning budget
- Home office stipend

## About Acme Analytics

We're a 50-person startup helping companies make sense of their data. We're
backed by top investors and growing 300% year-over-year.

Our team is fully remote and values:
- Shipping fast and iterating
- Clear communication and documentation
- Work-life balance (we mean it - no late-night deploys)
- Diversity of thought and background

## How to Apply

Apply at acme.com/careers with:
- Your resume
- GitHub profile or code samples
- Brief note about what interests you about this role

We review applications on a rolling basis and respond within 5 business days.

---

**Acme Analytics is an equal opportunity employer. We celebrate diversity and are
committed to creating an inclusive environment for all employees.**
```

## What Makes These Examples Good

1. **Clear compensation** - Transparent salary and equity ranges
2. **Specific responsibilities** - Not vague, shows day-to-day work
3. **Level-appropriate** - IC2 vs IC3 have different scope
4. **Inclusive language** - No rockstar/ninja, gender-neutral
5. **Reasonable requirements** - 3-4 must-haves, 2-3 nice-to-haves
6. **Company context** - Stage, size, values
7. **How to apply** - Clear next steps

Use these as templates and adapt to your company's voice and needs.
