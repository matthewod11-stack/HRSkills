# Mock Interview Guide Examples

Sample interview guides for common roles at TechCo.

---

## Senior Software Engineer (IC3) - Technical Interview

**Duration:** 60 minutes
**Interviewer:** Staff Engineer or Senior Engineer
**Focus:** System design and technical depth

### Questions

**1. System Design (30 min)**
"Design a URL shortener like bit.ly. How would you build it to handle 100M URLs and 1B clicks per month?"

**Assess:** Architecture, scalability, databases, caching, trade-offs

**Rubric:**
- 5: Comprehensive design, considers scale, trade-offs, edge cases
- 4: Solid design, scalable, explains decisions
- 3: Basic design, some gaps in scale or reliability
- 2: Incomplete or unscalable design
- 1: Cannot design working system

**2. Technical Deep-Dive (20 min)**
"Walk me through the most complex system you've designed. What were the challenges and how did you solve them?"

**Assess:** Technical depth, decision-making, learning from experience

**3. Code Quality (10 min)**
"How do you ensure code quality in a fast-moving team?"

**Assess:** Testing, code review, standards, pragmatism

## Product Manager (IC3) - Behavioral Interview

**Duration:** 45 minutes
**Interviewer:** Hiring Manager (Director of Product)
**Focus:** Product sense, stakeholder management, execution

### Questions

**1. Product Sense (15 min)**
"Tell me about a product you shipped that failed. What happened and what did you learn?"

**Assess:** Ownership, learning mindset, user empathy

**Rubric:**
- 5: Takes ownership, analyzes root cause, applied learnings
- 4: Honest about failure, learned from it
- 3: Some ownership, basic learnings
- 2: Blames others, defensive
- 1: No examples or learning

**2. Stakeholder Management (15 min)**
"Describe a time you had to influence engineering to prioritize something they didn't want to build."

**Assess:** Influence, collaboration, data-driven

**3. Execution (15 min)**
"Walk me through how you run discovery and validation for a new feature."

**Assess:** Process, rigor, user-centricity

## Account Executive - Sales Interview

**Duration:** 45 minutes
**Interviewer:** Sales Manager
**Focus:** Sales process, closing skills, coachability

### Questions

**1. Sales Process (15 min)**
"Walk me through your sales process from first touch to close. Use a recent deal as an example."

**Assess:** Methodology, qualification, discovery, closing

**Rubric:**
- 5: Structured process, consultative, data-driven, closed complex deal
- 4: Clear process, good discovery, closes deals
- 3: Basic process, transactional, smaller deals
- 2: No clear process, struggles to close
- 1: Cannot articulate sales approach

**2. Objection Handling (15 min)**
"Tell me about a deal you lost. What happened and how did you handle objections?"

**Assess:** Resilience, learning, objection handling

**3. Coachability (15 min)**
"Describe a time you received tough feedback from a manager. How did you respond?"

**Assess:** Growth mindset, receptiveness to coaching

## Engineering Manager (M3) - Leadership Interview

**Duration:** 60 minutes
**Interviewer:** Director of Engineering
**Focus:** People leadership, team building, delivery

### Questions

**1. People Management (20 min)**
"Tell me about a time you had to manage an underperforming engineer. What did you do?"

**Assess:** Coaching, performance management, empathy + accountability

**Rubric:**
- 5: Clear expectations, supportive plan, documented, outcome-focused
- 4: Addressed directly, provided support, improvement seen
- 3: Attempted to help, mixed results
- 2: Avoided or handled poorly
- 1: No experience or awareness

**2. Team Building (20 min)**
"How do you build high-performing teams? Give me a specific example."

**Assess:** Hiring, culture, development, retention

**3. Delivery (20 min)**
"Describe a time your team missed a critical deadline. How did you handle it?"

**Assess:** Ownership, problem-solving, learning

## Common Question Library

### Technical (Engineering)
- "Explain [technology] to a non-technical person."
- "What's your approach to debugging complex issues?"
- "How do you balance technical debt vs new features?"
- "Tell me about a technical decision you regret."

### Product Management
- "How do you prioritize features?"
- "Walk me through a successful product launch."
- "How do you handle disagreement with engineering?"
- "What metrics do you track and why?"

### Sales
- "How do you build pipeline?"
- "Walk me through a discovery call."
- "How do you handle a multi-stakeholder deal?"
- "What's your approach to negotiation?"

### Leadership (Managers)
- "How do you give difficult feedback?"
- "Tell me about a time you hired the wrong person."
- "How do you handle conflict within your team?"
- "What's your management philosophy?"

### Universal (All Roles)
- "Why are you looking for a new opportunity?"
- "What's your ideal work environment?"
- "Tell me about a time you failed."
- "What do you want to learn next?"
