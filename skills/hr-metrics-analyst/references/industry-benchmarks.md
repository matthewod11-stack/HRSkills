# Industry Benchmarks - HR Metrics

## Turnover Rates by Industry

### Technology & Software
- **Overall:** 13.2% annually
- **Engineering:** 11-14%
- **Sales:** 18-25%
- **High-growth startups:** 15-20%
- **Mature tech cos:** 8-12%

### By Company Stage
- **Series A-B:** 20-25% (high growth chaos)
- **Series C-D:** 15-20% (scaling challenges)
- **Pre-IPO/Public:** 10-15% (more stable)

## Hiring Velocity

### Time-to-Fill (Days)
- **Software Engineer:** 40-60 days
- **Senior Engineer:** 50-70 days
- **Engineering Manager:** 60-80 days
- **Product Manager:** 45-65 days
- **Sales Rep:** 30-50 days
- **Sales Leader:** 60-90 days
- **Marketing:** 35-55 days
- **G&A/Operations:** 25-45 days

### Factors That Increase Time
- Remote-only roles: +10-15 days (larger candidate pool)
- Senior/specialized roles: +20-30 days
- Competitive market: +15-25 days

## Cost-per-Hire

### By Source
- **Employee referrals:** $1,000-$3,000 (cheapest, best quality)
- **Internal recruiting:** $3,000-$5,000
- **Job boards:** $4,000-$7,000
- **Recruiting agencies:** $15,000-$30,000 (15-20% of base salary)
- **Executive search:** $30,000-$100,000+

### Average by Company Size
- **0-50 employees:** $2,000-$5,000
- **50-200 employees:** $4,000-$7,000
- **200-1000 employees:** $5,000-$10,000
- **1000+ employees:** $6,000-$12,000

## Span of Control

### By Role Type
- **Engineering ICs:** N/A (no direct reports)
- **Engineering Manager:** 5-8 reports (sweet spot: 6-7)
- **Senior EM/Director:** 20-40 people (2-3 managers + some ICs)
- **VP Engineering:** 50-150 people (4-8 managers)

### By Function
- **Product:** Wider spans (8-12) - less hands-on management
- **Sales:** Moderate spans (6-10) - coaching intensive
- **Operations:** Wider spans (10-15) - more process-driven
- **Executive team:** 5-8 direct reports to CEO

## Revenue & Productivity

### Revenue per Employee
- **SaaS (public):** $250K-$500K
- **Enterprise SaaS:** $300K-$600K
- **PLG (Product-Led Growth):** $200K-$400K
- **Services/consulting:** $150K-$250K
- **World-class:** $500K-$1M+

### Engineering Efficiency
- **Engineers per PM:** 5:1 to 8:1 typical
- **Engineers per Designer:** 8:1 to 12:1 typical

## Diversity Benchmarks

### Tech Industry (2024)
**Women in Tech:**
- Overall tech workforce: 25-30%
- Engineering roles: 20-25%
- Product roles: 35-40%
- Leadership roles: 20-25%
- Board seats: 25-30%

**Underrepresented Minorities (URM):**
- Overall tech workforce: 15-20%
- Engineering roles: 12-18%
- Leadership roles: 10-15%

### Startup Benchmarks by Stage
- **Early stage (0-50):** Often less diverse (founding team effect)
- **Growth stage (50-200):** 20-30% women, 10-20% URM
- **Mature (200+):** 25-35% women, 15-25% URM

## Performance Distribution

### Standard Bell Curve
- **Top performers (Exceeds):** 10-20%
- **Solid performers (Meets):** 65-75%
- **Improvement needed (Below):** 10-15%
- **Underperformers (Does not meet):** 5-10%

### Promotion Rates
- **Overall annual promotion rate:** 10-15%
- **High performers:** 20-30% promoted within 2 years
- **Average tenure to promotion:** 18-24 months

## Using These Benchmarks

**Remember:**
1. **Context matters** - Early stage vs. mature company
2. **Location matters** - SF vs. remote vs. international
3. **Industry matters** - SaaS vs. marketplace vs. fintech
4. **Not absolute rules** - Use as guides, not gospel
5. **Trends > snapshots** - Direction matters more than single point

**When analyzing:**
- Compare to similar companies (stage, size, market)
- Look at trends over time
- Understand the "why" behind numbers
- Focus on controllable metrics
