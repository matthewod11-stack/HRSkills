# 1:1 Question Bank

Comprehensive library of questions for different 1:1 scenarios.

---

## Opening/Check-In Questions

**How are you?**
- "How are you doing? Really - not just 'fine.'"
- "What's your energy level this week (1-10)?"
- "How was your weekend?"
- "Anything outside work affecting you right now?"
- "What's on your mind today?"

**Work State:**
- "How's your workload feeling?"
- "Anything stressing you out?"
- "What's going well?"
- "What's been hard?"

---

## Career & Growth

**Aspirations:**
- "Where do you see yourself in 2-3 years?"
- "What does success look like for you?"
- "If you could design your perfect role, what would it be?"
- "What kind of work makes you lose track of time?"
- "What would make you excited to wake up for work?"

**Development:**
- "What skills do you want to build?"
- "What are you learning lately?"
- "Who do you want to learn from?"
- "What would stretch you in a good way?"
- "What's one skill that would unlock the next level for you?"

**Progress:**
- "How's progress on [development goal]?"
- "What's helping you grow?"
- "What's blocking your growth?"
- "Do you feel like you're learning here?"

---

## Performance & Feedback

**Self-Assessment:**
- "What are you most proud of this week/month?"
- "What's one thing you'd do differently if you could redo it?"
- "Where do you feel strongest?"
- "Where do you want to improve?"
- "How do you think [project] went?"

**Manager Feedback (Asking for):**
- "What can I do better to support you?"
- "Am I giving you enough context?"
- "How can I help remove blockers?"
- "What am I not seeing?"
- "Do you feel like I have your back?"
- "What do you need more/less of from me?"

**Delivering Feedback:**
- "Can I share some feedback on [specific thing]?"
- "I noticed [behavior]. Can we talk about it?"
- "Here's what went really well: [specific example]"
- "Here's something to work on: [specific, actionable]"

---

## Engagement & Satisfaction

**Happiness:**
- "On a scale of 1-10, how happy are you at work?"
- "What's the best part of your job?"
- "What's the most frustrating part?"
- "What would make your work life better?"
- "What energizes you? What drains you?"

**Fulfillment:**
- "Do you feel like your work matters?"
- "Are you making the impact you want to make?"
- "What would make this role more meaningful?"

**Team Dynamics:**
- "How's the team doing?"
- "Anyone you'd like to work more/less with?"
- "Any team friction I should know about?"
- "Do you feel supported by the team?"

**Company:**
- "How do you feel about the company direction?"
- "Do you feel connected to our mission?"
- "What would you change about how we work?"

---

## Retention Questions

**Direct:**
- "What would make you leave?"
- "What keeps you here?"
- "On a scale of 1-10, how likely are you to be here in a year? Why?"
- "What would it take to make you stay long-term?"

**Probing:**
- "Are you getting what you need to thrive here?"
- "Do you feel valued?"
- "Are there opportunities elsewhere that tempt you?"
- "What would your dream job look like? How close are we?"

**Comparison:**
- "If you got an offer elsewhere, what would you compare it to?"
- "What would have to be true for you to turn down another offer?"

---

## Problem-Solving & Support

**Blockers:**
- "What's blocking you this week?"
- "What can I help unblock?"
- "Who do you need me to talk to?"
- "What decision do you need from me?"

**Challenges:**
- "What's the hardest part of your job right now?"
- "What's keeping you up at night?"
- "If you had a magic wand, what would you fix?"

**Resources:**
- "Do you have what you need to succeed?"
- "What tools/resources would make you more effective?"
- "What support are you missing?"

---

## Manager-Specific (for Managers)

**People Management:**
- "How's the team feeling?"
- "Any concerns about specific team members?"
- "Who's thriving? Who's struggling?"
- "How are 1:1s going with your reports?"

**Leadership:**
- "What decisions are you wrestling with?"
- "Where do you need my input vs autonomy?"
- "How can I help you be a better manager?"
- "What would you do if I weren't here?"

---

## Difficult Conversations

**Underperformance:**
- "I've noticed [specific behavior/issue]. What's your perspective?"
- "What's causing this?"
- "What support do you need to get back on track?"
- "Here's what needs to change: [specific, measurable]"

**Conflict:**
- "Can we talk about what happened with [person/situation]?"
- "I want to understand your side. Tell me more."
- "How would you like to see this resolved?"
- "What can we both do differently?"

**Burnout:**
- "You seem exhausted. What's going on?"
- "What's contributing to this?"
- "What would make this sustainable?"
- "What can I take off your plate?"

---

## Onboarding (30/60/90 Days)

**30 Days:**
- "How's onboarding going?"
- "What's been confusing?"
- "What's been great?"
- "Are you getting the support you need?"
- "What could we improve for the next new hire?"

**60 Days:**
- "Feeling more settled?"
- "What's clicking? What's not?"
- "How's the work compared to what you expected?"
- "Any surprises (good or bad)?"

**90 Days:**
- "How do you feel after 3 months?"
- "Ready to ramp up to full speed?"
- "What do you want to focus on next?"
- "Looking back, what advice would you give yourself from day one?"

## Skip-Level (Manager's Manager)

**Experience:**
- "How's it going working with [manager]?"
- "What's [manager] doing well?"
- "What could [manager] do better?"
- "Do you get what you need from [manager]?"

**Team:**
- "How's the team culture?"
- "Anything concerning I should know about?"

**Company:**
- "Do you have what you need to succeed?"
- "What should leadership know that we might not see?"

## Exit Interview (If Leaving)

**Why:**
- "What led to your decision to leave?"
- "What could we have done differently?"
- "What are you going to that we couldn't offer?"

**Feedback:**
- "What did we do well?"
- "What should we change?"
- "What would you tell someone considering working here?"

**Bridge:**
- "How can we stay in touch?"
- "Would you come back someday?"
- "How can we make your last [X weeks] great?"

## Question Selection Guide

**Choose questions based on:**

**Employee Level:**
- Junior: More guidance, concrete questions about work
- Senior: More open-ended, strategic, career-focused
- Manager: Leadership challenges, team health

**Situation:**
- Regular 1:1: Mix of check-in, work, growth
- Performance issue: Direct, specific, action-oriented
- Retention risk: Understanding motivations, addressing concerns
- New hire: Onboarding experience, clarity, support

**Relationship:**
- New relationship: Build trust, understand motivations
- Established: Deeper questions, challenges, growth

**Time:**
- 30 min: Focus on 3-4 key topics
- 60 min: Can do deep-dive on career, challenges

**Remember:**
- Ask fewer, better questions (not 20 rapid-fire)
- Listen more than you talk
- Follow up on previous conversations
- Don't ask questions you're not ready to act on
